About

My name is Bailey Schmidt, a video-game lover living in Thunder Bay, Ontario. I do many things such as website buiding, graphic design, animation, and photography but my true passion is 3D modelling and character design. I have experience in all of these and I love doing them all despite my love for 3D modeling. I spend most of my time either coming up with new ideas for projects, stories and sometimes just to draw.

I am often looking for new experiences, whether they be through travel, work, or my own projects. I am currently enrolled at Confederation College in Thunder Bay for Interactive Media Development.
